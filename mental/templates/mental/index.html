ReachOutKenya

Mental Health has become an international disaster and should be declared so by all Leaders, we cannot sit and watch as a generation swept off. It has and keeps on stealing the show; it has overtaken Cancer, AIDS, Malaria etc. We are reaching out to everyone in your own capacity to help instigate or demystify this.

Reach Out KE is a not for profit movement or foundation that has seen this menace and has decided no more sweeping things under the carpet. The country has very many mentors, health practitioners, counselors who are ready even to give services at subsidized rates and some as a CSR activity. All industries are affected. Case in scenario We get into public transport an d travel to destinations without really knowing who is behind the wheel and even if we know them physically , we don‟t really know their state of mind when at work. So it‟s all about REACHING OUT, In psychology you are able to tell mood through a greeting. “Hi, Vipi, Sasa, uko aje” seem just normal words but these words carry weight. Our Approach.

Programs

Substance abuse

We provide a range of prevention, treatment, and recovery services through Assessment and Diagnosis,Counseling and Therapy,Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT), Support Groups, Education and Prevention.Overall, we use a multifaceted approach to address substance abuse, with a focus on individualized treatment plans that take into account the unique needs and circumstances of each person.

Coping skills

We offer a range of services to help individuals develop coping skills which include Therapy,Support Groups, Workshops and classes,Online resources. Overall, we aim to help individuals develop coping skills that can help them manage stress, anxiety, depression, and other mental health challenges.

Aftercare program

We offer aftercare programs to help individuals maintain their mental health and well-being after completing a treatment program through Continuing therapy or counseling, Support groups, Medication management, Education and training, Crisis intervention. Our aftercare aims to help individuals maintain their mental health and well-being over the long term, and to provide them with the support and resources they need to achieve their goals.
